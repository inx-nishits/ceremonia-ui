Frequently Asked Questions

CeremonIA is an AI-powered platform that creates personalized narratives for your secular ceremony based on your anecdotes and memories. It transforms your unique love story into a beautifully crafted ceremony script.

After logging in with your credentials, you answer a guided questionnaire organized by blocks, either by typing or using voice dictation. You then choose the tone and writing style (e.g., romantic, poetic, or humorous) to personalize the atmosphere. The AI then assembles your anecdotes and choices to automatically generate a complete, ready-to-use ceremony narrative.
